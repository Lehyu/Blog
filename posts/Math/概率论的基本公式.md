1. Union:$p(A \lor B) = p(A)+p(B)-p(A \land B)$
2. Joint:$p(A,B)=p(A \land B)=p(A|B)p(B)$
3. $p(A)=\sum_{b}{p(A,B=b)}=\sum_{b}{p(A|B=b)p(B=b)}$
4. $p(X_{1:D})=p(X_{1})p(X_{2}|X_{1})p(X_{3}|X_{2},X_{1})\dots p(X_{D}|X_{1:D-1})$
5. 条件概率:$p(A|B)=\frac{p(A,B)}{p(B)}$ if $p(B)>0$
6. 贝叶斯公式: $p(X=x|Y=y)=\frac{p(X=x,Y=y)}{p{Y=y}}\frac{p(X=x,Y=y)}{\sum_{x^{'}}p(Y=y|X=x^{'})p(X=x^{'})}$
7. 无条件独立:$X \perp Y \iff p(X,Y) = p(X)p(Y)$
8. 有条件独立:$X \perp Y|Z \iff p(X,Y|Z)=p(X|Z)p(Y|Z)$
9. $X \perp Y|Z$ iff there exist function $g$ and $h$ such that $p(x,y|z)=g(x,z)h(y,z)$, for all $x,y,z$ and $p(z)>0$
